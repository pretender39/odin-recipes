Bolognese recipe

Description

This classic Bolognese recipe features a rich and savory meat sauce simmered with tomatoes, onions, and Italian herbs.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup finely chopped carrots
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 ½ cups whole milk, divided
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (6-ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add carrots, onion, and garlic; cook and stir until tender, about 10 minutes. Add ground beef; cook, stirring and breaking up meat, until browned, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain off excess fat from the skillet.
  2. Stir in 1 cup milk; bring to a simmer. Reduce heat to medium-low and cook until milk is absorbed, about 15 minutes.
  3. Transfer beef mixture to a slow cooker and set to High. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes. Cover and cook on High for 2 hours. Stir in remaining ½ cup milk and Parmesan; cook for 2 more hours.
